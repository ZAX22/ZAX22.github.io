Pokemon foundation

The franchise was created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1995.The franchise began as a pair of video games for the original Game Boy, developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo. The franchise now offers video games, trading card games, animated television shows and movies, comic books, and toys. In total all games have sold over 279million copies. 279MILLION COPIES. It was originally called "Pocket Monsters".
